Abudawood Group  ·  Executive Management  ·  Group-wide (all markets)

Job Summary

The Group Chief Executive Officer (Group CEO) will provide strategic leadership to drive business growth, expansion, and profitability across all markets. The Group CEO will be responsible for developing and implementing the company's overall vision, mission, and strategy, while ensuring alignment with the organization's values. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of success in leading large, complex organizations, with a deep understanding of the FMCG industry and the Middle East market. The Group CEO will be expected to build and maintain strong relationships with key stakeholders, including customers, suppliers, and employees.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement the company's overall strategy and vision
  • Provide leadership and direction to the executive management team
  • Drive business growth and expansion across all markets
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with key stakeholders
  • Oversee the development and execution of business plans and budgets
  • Monitor and analyze market trends and competitor activity
  • Identify and mitigate risks to the business
  • Ensure compliance with all relevant laws and regulations
  • Foster a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship
  • Develop and implement initiatives to improve operational efficiency and effectiveness
  • Lead and manage change initiatives across the organization

Education & Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Finance, or a related field
  • MBA or other advanced degree preferred
  • Relevant professional certifications (e.g. CPA, CMA)

Experience Requirements

  • Minimum 15 years of experience in a senior leadership role, preferably in the FMCG industry
  • Proven track record of success in driving business growth and expansion
  • Experience in managing large, complex organizations
  • Strong understanding of the Middle East market and cultural nuances
  • Experience in building and maintaining strong relationships with key stakeholders

Skills

  • Strong strategic thinking and planning skills
  • Excellent leadership and management skills
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to build and maintain strong relationships
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment

Behavioral Competencies

  • Strategic Thinking
  • Leadership
  • Collaboration and Partnership
  • Communication and Influencing
  • Adaptability and Flexibility
  • Results Orientation

Working Conditions

The Group CEO will be based in Saudi Arabia, with frequent travel to other markets in the Middle East. The role will require a high level of energy, flexibility, and adaptability, with the ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
